Kamal Budhathoki

My name is Kamal Budhathoki and I have been producing furniture since 2010. I work in Home Furiture, but delivery and assembly of products anywhere in Urlabari or outside is a natural part of my work. I make custom furniture in accordance with the customer's wishes. I specialize in kitchen cabinets, built-in wardrobes, wardrobes, beds, corridor walls, children's rooms, living room sets and furniture for schools and kindergartens.

WHAT DO I OFFER?

PROPOSAL

I provide a complete solution design based on inspection. Design, material selection, decor and 3D visualization in several versions, for your absolute satisfaction.

PREPARATION

Once the 3D design has been approved, I will draw mostly on the kitchen cabinets on the spot how to run installations such as water, sewage, electrical plugs for built-in appliances and also LED lighting.

PRODUCTION

I only use quality materials from the Austrian company Egger. All fittings are supplied with a lifetime warranty from Blum (Austria). As a standard, I use 2mm ABS edges for cabinet cabinets, drawers or built-in cabinets, which guarantees long product life.

MOUNTING

Of course there is a final assembly on site, which is included in the price of each order. Transport and removal of individual parts of furniture, functional testing and adjustment of all fittings during assembly.
